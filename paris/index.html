Pub Crawls in London

Visit London's best clubs in one night!

More info

What is it?

A pub crawl, sometimes known as a barathon, is a tour of different pubs and clubs in an area of your choice.

Who is it for?

For all ages from 18 up. All you need is your ID to join the party!

How does it work?

It all starts at the meeting point where we will provide you with a wristband that gives you access to all the clubs we will take you to.

Where do we meet?

Depending on your choice, the meeting point will be written on the ticket. Contact us if you have any questions.
